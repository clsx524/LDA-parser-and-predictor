Dead Hooker in a Trunk is a 2009 Canadian exploitation film starring, written, produced and directed by twin sisters Jen Soska and Sylvia Soska, also known as The Soska Sisters. The film is their feature film directorial debut and is distributed by IFC. The film features music by a variety of local Vancouver acts such as Craig James, Fake Shark - Real Zombie! and CJ Wallis.
Four friends set out on an everyday errand and end up in a fight for their lives when they find the body of a dead hooker in the trunk of their car.
