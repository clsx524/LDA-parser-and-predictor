This debut video release by Aaron Carter, preteen pop singer and younger brother of the Backstreet Boys' Nick Carter, features the music videos for five songs from Carter's 2000 CD Aaron's Party (Come Get It): "Iko Iko," "Clapping Song," "I Want Candy," "Bounce," and the title track "Aaron's Party (Come Get It)."
