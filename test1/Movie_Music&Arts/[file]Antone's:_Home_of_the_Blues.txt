When the biggest names in the blues come out to play, they always know that there's a good time to be had at Antone's. In this release, the history of the legendary Austin, Texas club is explored through detailed interviews with those who kept the music coming throughout the years. Featured performers include Buddy Guy, B.B. King, Stevie Ray Vaughn, Kim Wilson, and a whole host of talented blues musicians. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 99&nbsp;mins
