28 Hotel Rooms is an American feature film written and directed by Matt Ross and starring Chris Messina and Marin Ireland. It is Matt Ross' first feature film.
While traveling for work in a city far from their homes, a novelist and a corporate accountant conduct an affair over a period of several years. The film takes a minimal approach, and consists of scenes between the two of them in hotel rooms.
