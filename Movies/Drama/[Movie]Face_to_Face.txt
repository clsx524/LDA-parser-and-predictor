Content: Director Michael Rymer (Angel Baby), Queen of the Damned) teams with acclaimed Australian playwright David Williamson for this adaptation of Willaimson's topical play about bullying in the workplace. Upon being fired from his job in construction, sensitive Wayne (Luke Ford) destroys his boss' Jaguar. In the aftermath of that destructive outburst, Wayne is given the opportunity to avoid prosecution by discussing his actions in a community conference attended by his boss, his boss' wife, his colleagues, his best friend, and his weary mother. Now the more heated the conversation grows, the more complicated the situation becomes until it begins to have a major impact on the lives of all involved. Vince Colosimo, Sigrid Thornton, and Matthew Newton co-star.
