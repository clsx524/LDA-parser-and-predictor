Saltwater
Released Year: 2012
Running time: 81
Director: Charlie Vaughn
Cast: 
Content: Charlie Vaughn takes the reins to helm this low-budget LGBT drama. The story opens when Will - a homosexual officer in the U.S. Navy - leaves the armed forces, reconnectswith old friends, and begins to look for a new boyfriend. His buddy Rich attempts to fix him up with the sexy and handsome Josh; while chemistry sizzles between the two, their timetables can never quite align - at first. Then an unforeseen tragedy erupts that brings the two men together and forces them to respond in accord to the challenging situation before them.
