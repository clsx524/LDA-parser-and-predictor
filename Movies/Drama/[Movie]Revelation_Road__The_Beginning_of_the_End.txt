Content: Following in the steps of such movies as A Thief in the Night and Left Behind, this Christian-themed drama takes place in the days leading up to the New Testament's Book of Revelation. Josh McManus is a traveling salesman with a sketchy past. He arrives in a West Texas town and becomes implicated in a conflict with a tough biker gang called The Barbarians, with a particularly nasty leader called Hawg. Then, suddenly, chaos strikes everywhere, signaled by flashes in the sky and cataclysmic earthquakes. The Rapture is upon them, and Josh finds himself engulfed by a massive battle, transpiring on both earthly and heavenly levels.
