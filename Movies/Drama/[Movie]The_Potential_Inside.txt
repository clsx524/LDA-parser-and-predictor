The_Potential_Inside
Released Year: 2010
Running time: 95
Director: Scotty Curlee
Cast: 
Content: A dejected and washed-up athlete lands a shot at redemption in this gentle, earnest drama. After retired professional cyclist Chris Carmik (Scotty Curlee) experiences a tragic automobile accident, he sinks into a period of deep depression. Chris soon finds a chance to start over, however, via a young cycling prodigy named Jake (Michael Cuddire) - who he promptly inducts into the bicycle racing community. Bit by bit, the two hone Jake's skills, but Chris must work even harder to help Jake realize the full breadth of his potential for greatness.
