Lukewarm
Released Year: 2012
Running time: 97
Director: Thomas Makowski
Cast: 
Content: A troubled bartender learns that the only way he can build a healthy future is by first confronting his tragic past in this Christian-themed drama starring John Schneider). Luke (Jeremy Jones) was just a child when his father (Schneider) walked out on the family. Ever since that day, Luke's been locked on the path to self-destruction. Now the more hours Luke spends slinging drinks at the local bar, the further he drifts away from his faith and his loving girlfriend (Nichole Gale Anderson). Just when it seems as if Luke has lost control, however, a chance to reconcile with his estranged father offers an opportunity to lay the foundation for a truly happy future.
