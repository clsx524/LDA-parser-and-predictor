Sins
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 84
Director: Carlo Fusco
Cast: 
Content: One man's violent Mafioso past returns with a vengeance in this crime drama featuring Danny Glover and Michael Madsen. David is living in New York City and working for the volatile Don Fredrico when hazy visions of his violent early years in Sicily begin bubbling to the surface of his memory. There, he was a member of the notorious Sicilian Cortello family. But he incurred the wrath of the powerful Don Michele in an act of bitter betrayal. Now David is desperate to embrace his Italian roots. Fleeing the city by train, David sets out on a tragic collision course with Don Federico, and meets enigmatic priest Father Leonard (Danny Glover), whose own dark past bonds him to the mysterious Mafioso.
