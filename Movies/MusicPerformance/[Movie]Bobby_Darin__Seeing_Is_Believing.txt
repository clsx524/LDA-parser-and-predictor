Content: The compilation film Bobby Darin: Seeing is Believing compiles into one release a series of vintage performance numbers from legendary crooner Darin. Most of the segments originally aired on The Ed Sullivan Show or the short-lived variety program The Bobby Darin Show (which ran for four months on NBC in 1973). Tracks include: "Splish Splash," "Work Song," "Mame," "Cry Me a River," "Sweet Caroline," "Beyond the Sea," "Artificial Flowers" and "If I Were a Carpenter." The program also features a duet performed by Darin and Connie Francis, and a duet performed by Darin and Bobbie Gentry.
