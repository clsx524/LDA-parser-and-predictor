Content: For fans of Western Swing, the 1930s, 40s, and 50s were truly the golden era. In this release featuring performance footage of Tex Williams, Spade Cooley, Bob Wills, and Hank Thompson, music fans will most certainly get their fill of the Western Swing flavor as they move to some of the 20th Century's most durable tunes.
