Content: This musical documentary allows fans of the teen singing sensation to take a trip inside the pop star's world. Included is a behind-the-scenes look at what goes on in the studio and on stage with the young diva. Also included are performances of many of Aguilera's most popular hits, including, of course, "Genie In a Bottle."
