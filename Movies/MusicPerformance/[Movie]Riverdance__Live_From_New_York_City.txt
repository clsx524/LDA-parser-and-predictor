Riverdance:_Live_From_New_York_City
Released Year: 1997
Running time: 102
Director: John McColgan
Cast: 
Content: Riverdance, the acclaimed Irish music and dance spectacular, comes to the Big Apple in this live video recorded at New York City's Radio City Music Hall. The New York performance features several new pieces never before available on video, including "American Wake" and "Harbour Of The New World." This production stars Jean Butler, Colin Dunne and Maria Pages, along with a cast of 70 dancers and musicians.
