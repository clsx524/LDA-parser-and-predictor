Content: The singersongwriter who rocked music lovers with Marvelous 3 in the 1970s before producing radio-friendly grooves for such chart-toppers as Avril Lavigne and Pink returns to the stage for this power-pop concert captured live at Tokyo's famed Budokan Arena. With albums like Left of Self-Centered and Letters, Butch Walker offered a catchy hybrid of 1970s-era album-oriented-rock and 1990s-era indie rock. Now fans can sing along with Walker as he brings his innovative solo material to the stage and the cameras roll to catch every hard-rocking minute.
