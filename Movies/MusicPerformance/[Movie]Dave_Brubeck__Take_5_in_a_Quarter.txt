Content: For half-a-century genre-jumping jazz veteran Dave Brubeck has been redefining hip, and in this performance the man who would become the first jazz musician ever to make the cover of Time Magazine offers a satisfying collection of familiar classics and intoxicating new tunes. Few quartets display the remarkable energy and musicianship of Brubeck's group, and now fans can enjoy a live set by the band anytime they please with this release that offers Brubeck on piano, Randy Jones in drums, Michael Moore on bass, and Bobby Militello pulling double duty on the flute and the alto sax.
