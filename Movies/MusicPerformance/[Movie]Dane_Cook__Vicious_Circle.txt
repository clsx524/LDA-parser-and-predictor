Content: The concert film Dane Cook: Vicious Circle captures the popular comic delivering his observational humor to an enthusiastic hometown crowd in Boston, MA. Cook touches on a variety of topics including arguing with a significant other. This special originally aired in September of 2006 on Home Box Office a month before his feature film Employee of the Month hit theaters.
