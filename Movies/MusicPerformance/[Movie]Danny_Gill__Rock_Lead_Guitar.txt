Content: Musician's Institute instructor Bruce Buckingham brings his expansive universe of knowledge to the masses in an instructional release designed to give any aspiring musician all the tools needed to master the essentials of rhythm guitar. With over two decades of teaching experience, studio time with such musical icons as Helen Reddy and Michael Jackson, and enough stage experience for a lifetime, Buckingham drive home the importance of such concepts as rhythms, triads and seventh chords, inversions and alternate voicings, and pentatonic and double-stop fills.
