Content: In the home release Balanchine: Jewels The Ballet of the Opera national de Paris performs George Balanchine's Jewels ballet, live on stage. Balanchine choreographs the work to featured music by classical composers Gabriel Faure, Igor Stravinsky, and Pyotr Ilych Tchaikovsky. Paul Connolly handles musical direction, with set and costume design by Christian Lacroix.
