Content: Amalia Rodrigues: Spirit of Fado documents the life and career of the renowned singer. Rodrigues specialized in a particular style of Portuguese song known as Fado. She made this style popular throughout the world, and she became a legend in her native country. In addition to charting her life, the film offers performance footage of her delivering some of her most well-loved tunes.
