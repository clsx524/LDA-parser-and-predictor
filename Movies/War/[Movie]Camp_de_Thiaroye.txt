Camp_de_Thiaroye
Released Year: 1988
Running time: 152
Director: Thiermo Faty Sow
Cast: 
Content: Writerdirectors Ousmane Sembene and Thiermo Faty Sow based Camp de Thiaroye on their own experiences. During the FrenchAlgerian war, a group of black soldiers fight on the side of France. But when hostilities cease, they are detained by their own government in the titular prison camp before being sent home. While incarcerated, they begin to wonder if their cause -- actually their country's cause -- is truly worth it. Camp de Thiaroye was jointly financed by sources from Senegal, Algeria and Tunisia.
