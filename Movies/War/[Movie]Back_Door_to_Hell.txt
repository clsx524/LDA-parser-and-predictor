Back_Door_to_Hell
Released Year: 1964
Running time: 69
Director: Monte Hellman
Cast: 
Content: In this WW II actioner, three soldiers are assigned to gather desperately needed information about Japanese occupation in the Philippines as the US prepares a major attack. During their arduous jungle journey, the soldiers lose their radio transmitter. Now totally on their own, the two not only succeed with their mission, they also manage to capture a Japanese outpost.
