Content: Her father killed while attempting to track down an ancient treasure, adventure writer Lanting (Lin Chiling) races to acquire the missing map, and encounters wonders beyond her wildest fantasies. Lanting has always written about adventure, but she's never actually experienced it. But that all changes when her father perishes under mysterious circumstances and his priceless treasure map disappears. Now, with the help of her childhood friend Qiaofei (Jay Chou), who also worked as her late father's assistant, Lanting ventures into the deserts of Mongolia and learns that some supernatural legends are born of truth.
