Wiki info: 
A Dark Truth (also known as The Truth) is an action thriller film directed and written by Damian Lee, and produced by Gary Howsam and Bill Marks. Starring Andy Garcia, Forest Whitaker, Eva Longoria, and Kim Coates. It premiered at the 2012 Boston Film Festival and released theatrically in the United States on January 4, 2013.
Jack Begosian (Garcia) is an ex-CIA operative who becomes a political talk show host after retiring as a spy. He’s hired by a corporate whistleblower to expose her company’s cover-up of a massacre in a South American village.
