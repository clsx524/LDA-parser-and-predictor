Released Year: 2011
Running time: 90
Director: Michael Wright
Cast: 
Content: A ferocious Amsterdam gangster embarks on a violent quest for revenge in this dark action thriller featuring Michael Madsen. J.D. (Rick Sinkeldam) is an Amsterdam thug who lives without fear. Kidnapped by Thai gangsters and heavily drugged, he reveals sensitive information about notorious crime boss Gunter. Later, after staging a daring escape, J.D. returns to the Red Light District, where sexy stripper Monique (Alison Carroll) awaits. Now the harder he fights to regain his memory, the faster he must run to avoid the wrath of his underworld enemies. Meanwhile, CIA Agent Martin Keele (Madsen) prepares to blow the Amsterdam crime scene sky high.
