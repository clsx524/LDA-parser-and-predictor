Content: Three young detectives take on the biggest case of their career after receiving an invitation to South Africa as the popular series of adventure novels makes the leap to the big screen. As the adventure gets underway, the Three Detectives race to help a desperate young girl from Cape Town, and capture a ruthless treasure seeker in search of untold riches. Later, the sleuthing trio has a tense encounter with a mythical beast known as "The Tokolosh" that few people have actually seen and lived to tell the tale.
