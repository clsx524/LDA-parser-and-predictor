Content: The last of mankind wage a bloody war against the armies of the living dead in this low-budget horror film shot in Nashville, Tennessee. Three years after an apocalyptic war, an American soldier (David Elby) returns home to the south, where his wife has been killed and their daughter has vanished without a trace. Desperate to find her, he joins forces with a small band of survivors on the hunt for the religious fanatic who brought about the fall of their country. Now, in the face of certain death, one soldier will fight to find his only hope for the future, even if it means sacrificing himself so that she can live.
