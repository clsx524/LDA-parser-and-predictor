Entrance
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 84
Director: Patrick Horvath
Cast: 
Content: A lonely Los Angeles woman embraces nostalgia to fill the growing void in her life, blindly walking into a nightmare as she struggles to realize where everything went wrong. Suzy (Suziey Block) is just another lost soul adrift in the big city. Depressed over her dead end job as a barrista and envious of her carefree roommate Karen, she fails to notice the danger signs indicating that something truly horrifying is about to happen to her.
