100 Ghost Street: The Return of Richard Speck or Paranormal Entity 4: The Return of Richard Speck is a 2012 supernatural horror film and fourth installment in the Paranormal Activity copycat series, Paranormal Entity.
In 2010, paranormal investigators tried to film Richard Speck's ghost at the site of his heinous killing spree. The victims' families have finally released the footage that documents their last days.
