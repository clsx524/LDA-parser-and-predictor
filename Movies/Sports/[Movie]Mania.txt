Content: This intimate documentary tells the story of Harry Glickman, and how he came to fulfill his dream of becoming a professional sports team owner, when he pooled investor support and became the owner of the Portland Trail Blazers in 1970. The sometime creator of a minor hockey league and sports journalist explain his journey, with commentary from athletes, hometown heroes, and more.
