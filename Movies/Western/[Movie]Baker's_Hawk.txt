Baker's_Hawk
Released Year: 1976
Running time: 98
Director: Lyman D. Dayton
Cast: 
Content: Baker's Hawk is an old-style Western starring old-style Clint Walker. Burl Ives plays a recluse plagued by vigilantes. Ives is protected by Walker and his son, Lee H. Montgomery, on the basis of the lad's friendship with the old man. Baker's Hawk is based on a novel by Jack Bickham.
