Released Year: 1970
Running time: 90
Director: Sergio Martino
Cast: 
Content: Sergio Martio's first spaghetti western, Arizona Colt Returns is purportedly a sequel to Michele Lupo's Arizona Colt, though the cast has been almost entirely replaced. Anthony Steffen stars as the titular outlaw, who -- together with his sidekick, Whiskey (Roberto Camardiel) -- is on the run after being framed for a series of stagecoach robberies.
