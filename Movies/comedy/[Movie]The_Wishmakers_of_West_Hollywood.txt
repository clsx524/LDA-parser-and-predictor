Content: Three gay new arrivals to Southern California make a wish for true love while attempting to thrive in a town that's notorious for shattering dreams. Freshly out of the closet and eager to live life to its fullest, talented dancer Ben goes to work as a dance, Pilates, and yoga instructor. Love proves elusive, meanwhile, as gender-bending performance artist Cory becomes the personal assistant to an acerbic ex-television star (Sally Kirkland), and Jason reads screenplays for a actor who can't even make the D-list.
