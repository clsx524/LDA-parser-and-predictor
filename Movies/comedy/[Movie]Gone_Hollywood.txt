Gone_Hollywood
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 79
Director: Demetrius Navarro
Cast: 
Content: Fernando Carrillo, star of the popular telenovela Rosalinda, toplines this gentle comedy-drama. He stars as Al, a once great television star now washed up and unable to score another break. Tragedy strikes when his dad leaves him an enormous inheritance, but it comes with strings attached: the will stipulates that Al must return to his hometown and reopen his father's tavern. In the process, he reencounters old friends and a new dream girl.
