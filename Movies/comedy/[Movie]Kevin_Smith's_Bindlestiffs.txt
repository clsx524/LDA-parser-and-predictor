Content: Suspended from school on the heels of a trumped up graffiti charge, high school virgins John (John Karna), Luke (Luke Loftin) and Andrew (Andrew Edison) escape to the inner city determined to lose their innocence. Meanwhile, as the trio recklessly plunges themselves into the world of transients, prostitutes, and crackheads in an attempt to experience a real-life Cather in the Rye (despite having never actually read the literary classic), delusional security guard Charlie becomes convinced they're planning a high school massacre, and sets out on a mission to stop them at all costs.
