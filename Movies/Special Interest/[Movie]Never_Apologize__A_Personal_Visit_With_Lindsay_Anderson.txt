Never_Apologize:_A_Personal_Visit_With_Lindsay_Anderson
Released Year: 2007
Running time: 111
Director: Mike Kaplan
Cast: 
Content: When asked by the Edinburgh Film Festival to say a few short words about his late friend and colleague, the director Lindsay Anderson, Malcolm McDowell went a step further, crafting a warm, witty one-man monologue that ran for weeks in the UK. Director Mike Kaplan films McDowell's reminiscences and intercuts them with a wealth of archival, behind-the-scenes footage from such Anderson films as If, O Lucky Man and This Sporting Life.
