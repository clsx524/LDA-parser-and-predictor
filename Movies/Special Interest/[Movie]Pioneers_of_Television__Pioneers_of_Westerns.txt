Content: This documentary charts the history of one of the most popular genres in television, the western. The filmmakers discuss some of the classic oaters like The Big Valley, starring Barbara Stanwyck; The Wild West with Robert Conrad; and Gunsmoke, the longest running fictional series in TV history.
