Baker_Boys:_Inside_the_Surge
Released Year: 2010
Running time: 212
Director: Kern Konwiser
Cast: 
Content: Seasoned combat photographer Jon Steele turns his camera on an The Baker Company during a three-month deployment in Iraq that finds the battle-weary squadron doing everything in their power to gain the upper hand against a particularly fierce faction of Al Qaeda. Realizing that they will need much more than heavy firepower to defeat their resourceful and elusive enemy, the Baker Company resorts to such covert combat tactics as greasing the palms of tribal sheiks for information, and carefully attempting to gain the cooperation of Iraqi militias.
