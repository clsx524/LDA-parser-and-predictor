American_Experience:_Triangle_Fire
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 60
Director: Jamila Wignot
Cast: 
Content: This documentary examines a horrific fire that killed 146 factory workers when it engulfed the Triangle Shirtwaist Factory in New York City on March 25, 1911. The filmmakers explain how that particular factory had been a focal point in the battle for laborers to gain more rights on the job.
