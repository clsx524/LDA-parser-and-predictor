La_Bestia
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 93
Director: Pedro Ultreras
Cast: 
Content: In this documentary viewers learn the harrowing risks that Central American migrants take in order to reach America and start a new life. From the moment they reach the southern border of Mexico, it's a constant struggle for survival. By following a group of migrants atop a Mexican cargo train for two weeks, we witness their intense journey -- and their incredible determination to seek out a better future for their loved ones - firsthand.
