Content: National Geographic Explorer: Can the Gulf Survive? asks the biggest question following America's worst environmental disaster courtesy of BP. Cameras documented the first two months of cleanup after the spill as efforts expand from the water to land and air. Narrated by Peter Coyote, Environmental Protection Agency officials recount what happened as everyone tried to cap off the spill and save what was left of the landscape and its inhabitants.
