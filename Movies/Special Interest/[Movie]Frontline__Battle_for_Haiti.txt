Released Year: 2011
Running time: 60
Director: Dan Reed
Cast: 
Content: This release from the news magazine series Frontline takes a look at the devastating effects of the 2010 earthquake in Haiti - namely, the mass escape of hundreds of criminals from the decimated National Penitentiary. Revisiting the nation a year after the event, the filmmakers examine how the escaped cons have taken advantage of their second chance at freedom, reasserting their grip on the criminal underworld.
