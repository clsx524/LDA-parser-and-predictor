NOVA:_Emergency_Mine_Rescue
Released Year: 2010
Running time: 60
Director: Nick Evans
Cast: 
Content: This riveting documentary from the filmmakers at NOVA follows the stories of three of the teams who helped rescue the 33 Chilean miners who were trapped underground for 69 days following a collapse in 2010. Using the commentary and analysis of experts and engineers, and with animations and other recreations, the program explains the superhuman task of getting the workers out alive.
