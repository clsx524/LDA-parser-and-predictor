Content: This documentary takes a look at the Jamaican Railway System, a railroad that worked the Banana Boat Steam Train's 63 mile run until 1992. The documentary traverses its tracks, which climb to heights as impressive as 800 feet, and also takes a behind-the-scenes look at the movie The Mercenaries, which depicts the railroad.
