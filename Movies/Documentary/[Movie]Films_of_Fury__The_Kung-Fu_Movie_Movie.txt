Content: A kung-fu obsessed cartoon host takes viewers on a whirlwind history of chopsocky cinema in this lively documentary from martial arts movie expert Ric Meyers. Featuring footage from over 200 martial arts classics and clips of Jackie Chan and Jet Li at their absolute best, Films of Fury is both an ideal introduction for the uninitiated, and an exhilarating, informative retrospective for die-hard fans who've seen it all.
