Arabia
Released Year: 2010
Running time: 46
Director: Greg MacGillivray
Cast: 
Content: As a filmmaking student at Chicago's DePaul University, 22-year-old Hamzah Jamjoom returned to the Arabian Peninsula to craft this sweeping, ambitious documentary about his homeland. Chiefly designed as a tool to bridge different cultures and rectify vast misunderstandings between the West and Middle East, the film explores the roots of Arabian traditions and studies how Arabian society evolved over the course of history. It also examines the faith of the Arabian people and the cultural nuances of Arabia. The program is presented in a 3D format.
