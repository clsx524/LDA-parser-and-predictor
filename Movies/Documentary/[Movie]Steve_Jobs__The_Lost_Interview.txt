Released Year: 2011
Running time: 72
Director: Paul Sen
Cast: 
Content: In 1995 Robert Cringely sat down with Steve Jobs for an extensive interview about his life and career. Though only a snippet of the conversation was used at the time as part of a profile on Jobs, the entire interview was rediscovered after he passed away. This documentary presents the original interview in its entirety.
