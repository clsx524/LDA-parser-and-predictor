The_Undefeated
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 118
Director: Stephen K. Bannon

Content: Undefeated traces the political rise of Sarah Palin in Alaska before her explosion onto the national political scene when John McCain selected her to join him as the Vice Presidential candidate on the Republican Party's 2008 ticket. The film touches on the many social and political victories that at one point garnered her a whopping 80% approval rating as Alaska's governor.
