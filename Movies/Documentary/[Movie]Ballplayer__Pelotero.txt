Ballplayer:_Pelotero
Released Year: 2011
Running time: 77
Director: Ross Finkel

Content: Executive produced by Major League Baseball manager Bobby Valentine, this documentary examines the lives of two 15-year-old Dominican baseball prospects and details how Major League squads find raw talent in that rich baseball culture. These two players are approaching a momentous milestone, because when they turn 16 they are able to sign with an American ball club.
