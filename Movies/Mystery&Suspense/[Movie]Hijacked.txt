Hijacked
Released Year: 2012
Running time: 90
Director: Brandon Nutt
Cast: 
Content: A special agent risks his life to save his ex-fiance when the private jet she's traveling on is hijacked by the minions of a notorious crime boss. As the plane ascends, Special Agent Ross (Randy Couture) realizes that the same gangster he's been hunting plans to blow it out of the sky. With no time to lose, Agent Ross prepares to fight for his life, and the life of the woman he loves, at 30,000 feet. Dominic Purcell, Tiffany Dupont, and Vinnie Jones co-star.
