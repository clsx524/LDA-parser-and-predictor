Jester_Till
Released Year: 2003
Running time: 84
Director: Eberhard Junkersdorf
Cast: 
Content: Mischievous European folk hero Jester Till embarks on the adventure of a lifetime after his grandfather vanishes while developing a new potion with the power to grant total happiness. Jester's grandfather Marcus has sensed that the people of his town are unhappy. But just as Marcus is close to perfecting a potion that will make all of their worries vanish into thin air, his lab is rocked by a massive explosion. Now, the only way for Jester to save the locals from drowning in their own despair is to seek out Marcus and bring him back home. It won't be easy, but fortunately Jester is always up to a challenge.
