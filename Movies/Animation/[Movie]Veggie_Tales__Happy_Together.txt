Content: This collection for children stresses the value of family, friendship, and faith. The three episodes include are "Sheerluck Holmes and the Golden Ruler", in which Sheerluck Holmes exercises his deductive reasoning skills; The Grapes of Wrath, which follows an asparagus whose feelings take a beating thanks to a bunch of grumpy grapes; and Esther, The Girl Who Became Queen, in which Esther must find the courage to do what's right as the world watches expectantly.
