LeapFrog:_Math_Adventure_to_the_Moon
Released Year: 2009
Running time: 36
Director: Bob Nesler
Cast: 
Content: This animated, educational release for kids ages three to six stars Tad and Lily, a couple of frogs who get some magic assistance from their good friend Edison when they need help with their latest math assignment. The story takes the amphibian students to the moon, and walks kids through a series of fun introductory math lessons on sorting and counting, through games and songs.
