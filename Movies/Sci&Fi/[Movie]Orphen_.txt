Content: This fantasy tale chronicles the journey of Kiridanshera, who forsakes his name to be called Orphen, after the elders of his society turn their back on his friend who was transformed into a dragon during an accident involving a magical sword. Orphen searches for his friend who, now in dragon form, is called Bloody August. His task grows in scale as enemies emerge over the course of his mission...including the very elders who betrayed him.
