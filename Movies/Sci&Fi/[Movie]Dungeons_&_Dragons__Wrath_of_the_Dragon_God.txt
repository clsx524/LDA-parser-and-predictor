Content: Put down that 24-sided die and return to the fantastic world of Dungeons & Dragons in this magical adventure from director Gerry Lively. A black dragon has gone on a rampage, but this is no ordinary dragon. Controlled from afar by an evil wizard using a magical orb, this dragon can only be defeated by defeating its nefarious master. As a sorceress and a warrior combine their strength to journey to the wizard's castle and put an end to the black dragon's reign of terror, the road to peace is filled with deadly obstacles at every turn.
