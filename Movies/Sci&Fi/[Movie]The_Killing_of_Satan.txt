The_Killing_of_Satan
Released Year: 1986
Running time: 95
Director: Efren C. Pinon

Content: In this Filipino-made supernatural tale, a man is chosen by his dead uncle to battle the forces of evil. He fights a being called "The Prince of Magic," who shoots rays from his hands, makes heads spin around and hypnotizes people. He finally faces Satan himself and engages him in a battle of magic.
