Westender
Released Year: 2003
Running time: 103
Director: Brock Morse
Cast: 
Content: A drunken wanderer with little direction and even less money, Lord Asbrey of Westender finds a new lease on life when his prized possession -- a ring that connects his past with his future -- is stolen from him. Asbrey's life lost all meaning some time ago, and now the only hope for redemption in his otherwise bleak existence lies in the prospect of recovering the one thing that matters most to him at any cost. As Asbrey is thrust into an uncertain future, the past returns to confront him in a way he never thought possible. Will Asbrey somehow summon the strength to face those who wronged him and take back what is rightfully his?
