Content: The enigmatic Incan city of Machu Piccu is explored in this release that expands on explorer Hiram Bingham's groundbreaking 1911 discovery to ponder the true purpose and function of the spectacular city located high in the Andes Mountains. Numerous archeologists have dedicated their careers to discovering the true role this esoteric city played in ancient Incan society, and as the men and women who hunger for truth wander the awe-inspiring ruins in search of a definitive answer, viewers can stand right alongside them thanks to The History Channel.
