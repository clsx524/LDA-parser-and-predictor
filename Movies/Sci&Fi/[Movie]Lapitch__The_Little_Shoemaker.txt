Lapitch:_The_Little_Shoemaker
Released Year: 1999
Running time: 75
Director: Milan Blazekovic
Cast: 
Content: This animated musical runs approximately 75 minutes and takes viewers on adventure after adventure with the big-hearted little mouse, Lapitch. Lapitch works as an apprentice for a cranky shoemaker and his loving wife. When Lapitch is thrown out for someone else's mistake, he and his faithful dog set out to see the world. He makes fast friends with a cute little circus mouse, Lisa, and matches wits with the evil Dirty Rat. This movie was made in Croatia and dubbed in English.
