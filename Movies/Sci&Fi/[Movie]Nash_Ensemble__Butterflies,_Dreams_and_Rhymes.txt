Content: In the children's musical fantasy Nash Ensemble: Butterflies, Dreams and Rhymes, a young American girl named Kip visits a strange Scottish castle to view and study a collection of paintings. Upon arrival, she is greeted by a magical, singing butterfly, who leads Kip and her friends on a series of colorful adventures from painting to painting, set to such favorite children's songs as "Rub-a-dub-dub, Three Men in a Tub," "Hush Little Baby" and "Three Blind Mice." The members of the Nash Ensemble of young vocalists star in the lead roles.
