Lunopolis
Released Year: 2009
Running time: 98
Director: Matthew Avant
Cast: 
Content: A pair of enterprising filmmakers investigate allegations that human beings are living on the moon and determining our fates down on Earth, and discover the conspiracy theories may be dead on in writerdirector Matthew J. Avant's ambitious indie sci-fi thriller. Following their cryptic leads to a Louisiana swamp that houses a secret agency called "The Church of Lunology", the filmmakers discover just how far the clandestine group will go in order to safeguard the secret that lingers just outside of our atmosphere, and could provide the secrets to eternal life.
