Content: That old familiar tale of duel personalities gets a flamboyant twist in this horror-themed musical told in the tradition of The Rocky Horror Picture Show and Phantom of the Paradise. Directed by Andre Champagne and featuring words and music by Alan Bernhoft, this version Robert Louis Stevenson's influential frightener follows a brilliant scientist as he creates an elixir that unleashes his raging, singing id.
