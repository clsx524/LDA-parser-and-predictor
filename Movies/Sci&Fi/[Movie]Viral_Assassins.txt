Content: A menacing epidemic is the centerpiece of this this gory action thriller from the schlockmeisters at Troma. Viral Assassins is set in a future society where those infected with a highly infectious disease are killed by government agents in order to keep them from further spreading the illness. When one of the government's own contracts the microscopic killer, it's a race against time to find a cure. Jim Gordon and Ray Kelly star.
