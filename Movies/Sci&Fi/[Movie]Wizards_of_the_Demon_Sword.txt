Wizards_of_the_Demon_Sword
Released Year: 1992
Running time: 90
Director: Fred Olen Ray
Cast: 
Content: This low-budget Troma film makes fun of low-budget sword and sorcery movies. The story centers on the battle for the mythical Sword of Aktar and its kidnapped keeper Ulric. But for a few stop-motion prehistoric creatures, the special effects are less than stellar.
