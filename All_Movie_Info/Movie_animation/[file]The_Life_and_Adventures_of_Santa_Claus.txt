Based on the works of author L. Frank Baum, this program offers a glimpse at the magical origins of the man that children from across the globe have come to know as Santa Claus. Beginning with his unique childhood, the story chronicles how Santa discovered his passion for making children happy, how he became a toymaker, and how his famous reindeer-pulled sleigh came to be, along with other beloved holiday traditions. ~ Tracie Cooper, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 76&nbsp;mins
