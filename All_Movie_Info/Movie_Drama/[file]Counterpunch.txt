A talented young boxer with Golden Gloves aspirations must turn over a new leaf following a stint in jail if he ever hopes to reach his true potential, and win the championship title. In a world where second chances are hard to come by, this struggling pugilist will have to wage the fight of a lifetime before he even sets foot into the ring.
