Pocahontas screenwriter Bruce M. Morris makes his feature directorial debut with this animated 3D religious drama about three lost souls who encounter their guardian angels during Catholic Mass. After the death of her husband, widowed mother Monica worries that she won't be able to maintain a stable home for her young son Diego. Meanwhile, elderly Dona Cata longs for peace in her golden years, and troubled bus driver Don Chema frets for the future of his son following a grim medical diagnosis. Later, as their guardian angels begin to materialize, all three begin to realize just how blessed they truly are. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 69&nbsp;mins
