A Perfect Ending is a 2012 LGBT film written and directed by Nicole Conn.  
What starts as a comedy of errors ends up a uniquely erotic journey. Rebecca (Barbara Niven) has a very unusual secret, one that not even her best friends know about. The last person on earth she expects to reveal it to is a high priced escort named Paris (Jessica Clark).
A woman begins a carnal journey of self discovery after a high-class call girl reveals her deepest, darkest secret in this gay-themed drama featuring Barbara Niven, Morgan Fairchild, and John Heard. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 106&nbsp;mins
