In 2010, the Chicago Blackhawks ended a nearly 50 year draught by brining Lord Stanley's Cup back to the Windy City. This documentary features numerous highlights from throughout that memorable season, and offers glimpses of Duncan Keith, Dustin Byfuglien, and Patrick Kane making some of the year's most memorable plays. ~ Perry Seibert, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 350&nbsp;mins
