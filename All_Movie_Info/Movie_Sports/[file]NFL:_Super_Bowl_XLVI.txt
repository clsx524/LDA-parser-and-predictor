The 2011-2012 New York Giants caught fire at the end of the season, skyrocketing after a 7-7 start to 6 straight wins concluding with a victory over the New England Patriots in Super Bowl XLVI. This documentary serves up highlights from throughout the season, including footage from the championship game, and the presentation of the Lombardi Trophy. ~ Perry Seibert, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 120&nbsp;mins
