This release contains complete game broadcasts of four celebrated wins in the history of baseball's Seattle Mariners. The content includes the 1995 one-game playoff win, the game 5 ALDS win over the Yankees, their game 3 victory of the White Sox in the 2000 ALDS, and a record-breaking win over the Angels in September of 2001. ~ Perry Seibert, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 703&nbsp;mins
