Mania (Greek: Μανία) is a 1985 Greek adventure film directed by Giorgos Panousopoulos. It was entered into the 36th Berlin International Film Festival.
This intimate documentary tells the story of Harry Glickman, and how he came to fulfill his dream of becoming a professional sports team owner, when he pooled investor support and became the owner of the Portland Trail Blazers in 1970.
