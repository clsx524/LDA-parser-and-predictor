Filmmaker Ron Vignone turns his lens on Slamdance Film Festival founder Jon Fitzgerald, a an aspiring professional golfer who signs on to The Golf Channel's Amateur Tour in a bid to improve his game, and take the top prize at the national Championships in Orlando, Florida. Along the way, Fitzgerald realizes that the skills required to be success in his chosen sport and the skills that will help him live a more fruitful, productive life are one and the same. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 87&nbsp;mins
