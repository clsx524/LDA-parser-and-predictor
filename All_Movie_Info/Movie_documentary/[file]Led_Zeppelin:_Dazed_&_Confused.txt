This sweeping documentary of the rock band Led Zeppelin attempts to tell the complete story of the band starting with their early days, and following them through their ascendency to the top of the rock world. The filmmakers feature extensive archival footage including performances of favorites like "Whole Lotta Love," "Misty Mountain Hop," and "Dazed and Confused." ~ Perry Seibert, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 53&nbsp;mins
