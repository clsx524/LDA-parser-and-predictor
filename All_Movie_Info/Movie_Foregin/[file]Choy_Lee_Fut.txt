Choy Lee Fut known as Fight the Fight in the USA is a 2011 Hong Kong action film directed by Tony Law and Sam Wong, starring Sammo Hung, Yuen Wah, Sammy Hung, Kane Kosugi and Stephen Wong, with special appearances by Lau Kar-wing and Dennis To.
A devoted son fights for his father's past and his own future in this martial arts adventure from director Wong Ming Sing. Upon returning home to rebuild his life, the son is shocked to learn that his father is planning to sell his martial arts school to a rival teacher.
