Emergency room nurse Kaitlyn (Natalie Zea) begins to experience the memories of a murder victim after being electrocuted by a defibrillator while attempting to save her life. Meanwhile, after failing to convince her friends and family that she's not going insane, Kaitlyn becomes determined to catch the killer before he can strike again, and gets some much needed help from veteran detective Peter Rafferty (Sean Patrick Flanery).
