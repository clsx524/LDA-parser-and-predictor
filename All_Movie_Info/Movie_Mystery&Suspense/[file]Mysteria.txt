Mysteria is a 2011 American thriller film directed by a Swiss director, Lucius C. Kuert, and starring Robert Miano, Billy Zane, Danny Glover, Meadow Williams and Martin Landau.
A once famous and now a washed-up Hollywood screenwriter fighting to finish his latest script with an unrealistic deadline. He finds himself in the center of a murder investigation involving a prominent politician's wife. The surrounding events feed him inspiration for his script.

Danny Glover, Martin Landau and Billy Zane topline this mind-bending {\thriller] from Swiss director Lucius Kuert. The premise concerns a down-on-his-luck Hollywood scribe struggling to finish his latest script under the burden of an impending deadline and a serious case of writer's block. He soon finds himself thrust into a situation wilder than any he has ever written about, as he becomes the chief suspect in a murder investigation involving the slaying of a politician's wife.
