As Israel and Iran brace for war, Senior FBI Agent Shane Daughtry (David A.R White) races against time to locate seven suitcase nukes that have been smuggled into America, and could prove the key to unleashing Armageddon. With the help of a retired arms dealer (Lee Majors), a free-thinking ex-Mossad agent (Stacy Keach), and a no-nonsense CIA Deputy Director (Randy Travis), Agent Daughtry must track down the elusive devices, and prevent them from being detonated on American soil.
