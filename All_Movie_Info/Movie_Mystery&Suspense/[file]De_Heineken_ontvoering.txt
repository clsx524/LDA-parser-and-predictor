Set in 1983, this Dutch crime thriller from director Maarten Treurniet dramatizes events surrounding the notorious kidnapping of beer mogul Alfred Heineken (here played by Rutger Hauer). When several hotheaded criminals with a serious grudge against the wealthy entrepreneur decide to abduct him with a demand for 35 million Dutch guilders, a vicious power struggle ensues, followed by Heineken's revenge against the perpetrators. ~ Nathan Southern, RoviSee All De Heineken ontvoering Trailers and ClipsTheatrical Feature Running Time: 123&nbsp;mins
