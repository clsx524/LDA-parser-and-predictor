Alien Dawn is an AmericanCanadian Science fictionComedy series on Nicktoons. The series premiered on February 22, 2013.
Filming began in August 2012 in New York City. It will be the first live-action series created for and by the American cable channel Nicktoons. A total of 26 episodes has been ordered for season one.
Alien Dawn follows the adventures of 16-year-old Cameron Turner as he races to uncover clues of a mysterious conspiracy hidden in the animated pages of a comic book series created by his missing father.
