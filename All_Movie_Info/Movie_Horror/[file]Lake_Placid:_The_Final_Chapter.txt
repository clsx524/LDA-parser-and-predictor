Lake Placid: The Final Chapter (also known as Lake Placid 4 or Lake Placid 4: The Final Chapter) is a 2012 made-for-television sequel to the film Lake Placid by Syfy. It was released on September 29, 2012. It is the fourth installment in the Lake Placid series. It was released on DVD on February 19, 2013.
