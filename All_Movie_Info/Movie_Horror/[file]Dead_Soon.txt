The survivor of a vicious attack maintains a fragile grip on sanity while struggling to prove that she was saved by a mysterious unseen force in this enigmatic psychological thriller. Alena was jogging on a mountain trail when a man came out of nowhere, and overpowered her near an abandoned well. Just when it seemed as if Alena was at the mercy of a madman, however, an unseen entity came to her rescue. But no one believes Alena's incredible story, and now the harder she tries to prove it, the further she continues to slip into a dark void of madness. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 66&nbsp;mins
