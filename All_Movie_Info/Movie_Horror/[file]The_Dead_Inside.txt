A burned-out wedding photographer and an author wrestling with writer's block go to gruesome lengths in order to keep their love alive in this musical horror comedy from director Travis Betz (Lo, Sunday). As the thought of shooting another wedding makes Wes (Dustin Fasching) want to hang up his camera for good, his girlfriend Fi (Sarah Lassez) seeks the inspiration to finish her latest zombie novel. But neither can muster any creativity, and when Fi starts to grow emotionally unstable, Wes vows to stay by her side. Fi is slowly being taken over by a dark entity, and as Wes fights to help her regain possession of her body, both find that inspiration can strike under the oddest of circumstances. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 98&nbsp;mins
