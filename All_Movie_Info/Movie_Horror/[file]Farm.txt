This apocalyptic thriller stars Michael Hoptop and Freddie Meyer as brothers who spend every day hard at work on their land, raising crops, tending to animals, and taking care of their house. Their lives have become nothing but this endless routine of work, and they notice that their crops as well as their animals are dwindling. When a distraught, disheveled woman appears at their door, one of the brothers is finally tempted to leave the farm. ~ Perry Seibert, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 72&nbsp;mins
