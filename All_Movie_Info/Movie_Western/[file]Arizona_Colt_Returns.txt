Sergio Martio's first spaghetti western, Arizona Colt Returns is purportedly a sequel to Michele Lupo's Arizona Colt, though the cast has been almost entirely replaced. Anthony Steffen stars as the titular outlaw, who -- together with his sidekick, Whiskey (Roberto Camardiel) -- is on the run after being framed for a series of stagecoach robberies. ~ Carly Wray, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 90&nbsp;mins
