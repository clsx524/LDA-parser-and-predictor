A vengeful ex-con searches for the people who had him wrongly imprisoned while seeking the gold he was accused of stealing in this faith-based western starring Alan Autry. Released from prison in 1878 after serving time for a crime that he didn't commit, Jake Kincaid vows to settle the score with the scoundrels who ruined his life.
