Gospel great Hezekiah Walker lights up the stage of the Hylton Memorial Chapel in Woodbrdge, Virginia in this performance that finds the soulful singer joined by The Love and Fellowship Crusade Choir. In addition to a nine-song performance featuring such spiritual songs as "Power Belongs to God", "Patiently Waiting", and "I've Got a Reason", a bonus music video for the song "Let's Dance" is also included. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 82&nbsp;mins
