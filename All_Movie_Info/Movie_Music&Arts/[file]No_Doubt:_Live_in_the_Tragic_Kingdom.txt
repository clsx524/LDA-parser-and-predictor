Originally filmed in Anaheim, California, during No Doubt's Tragic Kingdom tour, this video features Gwen Stefani and long-term bandmates Tony Kanal, Adrian Young, and Tom Young in a live concert performance. The 92-minute, 17 track video features fan favorites "Just a Girl", "Don't Speak", and "Spiderwebs", as well as lesser known selections like "End it On This" and "The Imperial March".  ~ Tracie Cooper, RoviComplete Cast of No Doubt: Live in the Tragic KingdomNo DoubtCategories:Music & Performing ArtsLooking for special editions of No Doubt: Live in the Tragic Kingdom?See All Versions
