Join legendary comic Cheech Marin as he returns to the stage alongside an all-star collection of the hottest comics on the stand-up circuit in this release of a hilarious performance captured live on stage in March of 2006. Originally aired on Comedy Central in an abbreviated, thirty-minute format, this release presents the uncensored, hour-long performance just as it was meant to be seen. Absolutely nothing is sacred when Marin and friends set their sights on the most outrageous aspects of Hispanic and American culture, and from the east coast to the west coast, everyone is sure to find something to laugh about as Marin makes his historical return to stand up comedy. ~ Jason Buchanan, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 90&nbsp;mins
