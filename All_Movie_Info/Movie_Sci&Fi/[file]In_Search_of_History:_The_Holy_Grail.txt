In Search of History: The Holy Grail documents the many legends that have sprung about the cup Jesus drank from during The Last Supper. The film examines many of the different clues that have surfaced throughout history concerning where the grail may have been transported and where it might be today. Also discussed are the rumors of magical powers the grail bestows upon those who have possessed it. ~ Perry Seibert, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 50&nbsp;mins
