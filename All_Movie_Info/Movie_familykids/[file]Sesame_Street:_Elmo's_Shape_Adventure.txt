Featuring guest appearances from actor James Blunt and the Pilobolus dance group, this program follows Sesame Street's beloved Elmo as he searches far and wide for an octagon in order to help a friend (Andy Samberg) in need. The show is packed with animation, songs, and basic mathematic principles designed to help prepare young children for further education. ~ Tracie Cooper, RoviTheatrical Feature Running Time: 45&nbsp;mins
