Hosted by Andy Cohen, this SNL showcase takes a look back at the women responsible for some of the show's funniest characters including Debbie Downer, Mary Katherine Gallagher, Oprah, and many more. The program includes footage from the original season of SNL, which featured Gilda Radner, Larraine Newman, and Jane Curtin, as well as more recent comediennes such as Kristin Wiig, Rachel Dratch, and Molly Shannon. ~ Tracie Cooper, RoviSee All Saturday Night Live: The Women of SNL Trailers and ClipsTheatrical Feature Running Time: 96&nbsp;mins
